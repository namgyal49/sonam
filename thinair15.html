our story
Description
Since 2010, at thinairadventure, we’ve been offering memorable experiences for people like you – excited about exploring Inspiring ladakh. We love our place and we are always proud and happy to show you its wonders. We are a culture of passionate travel professionals, with a focus on hospitality, inspiring experiences, safety and consistency. After thousands of private tours with visitors from all around the world, in 2012 we created the first ever shared tour from leh ladakh – the Two Castles in One Day. This extraordinary experience got highly appreciated by our guests and we would love to share it with you. thinairadventure currently operates many small-group shared tours and more private tours from manali,srinagar to almost every corner of ladakh. Furthermore, we are ready to customize any program to fit your schedule and travel preferences.
OUR VISION

To proudly present our beautiful himalaya to people all over the world..

OUR MISSION
To become the first choice for people visiting ladakh by providing reliable, quality & friendly travel services. To contribute to the development of the local communities and to protect the natural environment of the leh ladakh and regions we operate in..
